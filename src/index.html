About Task Net

Task Net is a lightweight task manager designed to help you plan your day, stay organized, and complete your tasks without distractions. Everything is stored locally on your device, so your data stays private and loads instantly.

How it works

  • Create tasks with a title, due date, and time.
  • Choose a project (Daily, Work, Travel, Education, or your own).
  • Tasks auto-sort into Today, Tomorrow, and All.
  • Add subtasks inside each task using the checklist.
  • Overdue tasks are highlighted so you can review them.

How to use

  • Click Sign Up to create your local profile.
  • Click Log In to continue your tasks.
  • Select a task to open its checklist panel.

Note: This is a demo app. No server, and no data leaves your device.
